  1. We find our customers the products they need

    If a customer needs to buy a product through IRINGA MULTISALES NETWORK, for example a farm, plot, residential house, business house, machine, tractor, touring car or business car, etc. all you have to do is come to our office and tell us what you need and we will tell you the price the tool you need, (we will tell you the price of a new tool or an old tool). If it is a farm, we will ask you the type of farm you want (a clean farm that is already cultivated or a new farm that is bush). If you need a plot, we will ask you, do you need the plot in the middle of town or on the edge of town?

    Once you tell us what you need, we will tell you the price, if you are satisfied, we will tell you the payment for the work we will do for you (which is usually ten percent (10%) of the cost of the product you want to buy through Iringa Multisales Network). Once we agree, we will give you a bank account to pay for; and once you have paid, you will return to our office with a copy of the bank transfer paper and leave to us, the original copy of the bank transfer paper that you will keep yourself. Then we will tell you when to come so that we can hand over your product to you, it could be after a week, two or a month depending on the type of product you need. We can also work for you by finding your need according to the value of your money, and in our activities, at every step we must involve our client.
