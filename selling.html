  1. We Sell Our Clients' Properties on Their Behalf

    In the community there are many people who need to sell houses, cars, fields, plots, machines etc. but they do not know where they will find customers. Now our company is the savior for such people. If you have a product that you want to sell through our company, all you have to do is come to our office with documents that give you legal ownership; we will review the documents and if we are satisfied we will enter into a contract of agreement, after closing the contract of agreement, you will leave us a copy of the deed of the house, plot, farm or car card.

    After that, the office will carry out the process of finding a customer to sell your product. Once we sell your product, we will deduct the payment according to the value we sold your product as we agreed in the contract (usually it is ten percent (10%) of the sales value of your product). We hand over the remaining amount to the owner.
